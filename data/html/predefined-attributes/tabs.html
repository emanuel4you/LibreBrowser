tabs

DCL Attributes

tabs = "string";

Set up these tab stops.

This attribute defines the spacing of tabs, which is beneficial for arranging text into vertical columns.

string: a string of real numbers or integers, each separated by a space.

EXAMPLE

tabs = "5 10 15 20 25 30 35 40";
